Hide Sticky and Promote
=======================

From .info description: This module hides the "promote to front page" and"sticky" checkboxes when creating and editing content. This is to avoid confusion for our content editor since we do not use those options.

(thanks to Roger Codina and his fork: https://github.com/rogercodina/hide-sticky-promote and Elijah Lynn's post at http://drupal.stackexchange.com/questions/4612/how-to-disable-promote-to-front-page-and-sticky-options)
